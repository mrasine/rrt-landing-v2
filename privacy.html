Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-02

Overview

Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas ("RRT") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you schedule a trial, complete forms, or communicate with us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details: name, email, phone number
  • Child data: athlete name, date of birth, age category
  • Form responses: waiver form data (medical and liability acknowledgments)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm trial bookings and send reminders
  • To process and store signed waivers securely
  • To communicate class updates and important notices

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent your data
  • We may share information with authorized staff and coaches for class management
  • We may share information with emergency services or medical professionals if required by law

4. Data Security

  • We store information in secure, access-controlled systems
  • Only authorized RRT personnel may access your personal data

5. Children's Privacy

Because our programs serve children, we collect limited information about minors (name, date of birth, age category) as necessary to maintain class records, assign appropriate age groups, and meet our legal obligations for risk assessments and accident reporting. A parent or legal guardian must provide consent before any child's data is collected. We take great care of children's information, and only authorized personnel who need it as part of their role will have access. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you believe we have collected data about a child without proper authorization, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

6. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website, analyze site traffic, and understand how visitors interact with our pages. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the site.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Waiver and registration data is retained for the duration of your child's enrollment and for a reasonable period afterward for legal and safety record-keeping. You may request deletion of your data at any time, subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain certain records.

8. Your Choices

You may request to review, update, or delete your data at any time by contacting us at rhythmicribbonoftexas@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will post changes with the updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Texas, without regard to conflict of law principles.
