Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-07

Overview

Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas ("RRT") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you schedule a trial, complete forms, or communicate with us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details: name, email, phone number
  • Child data: athlete name, date of birth, age category
  • Form responses: waiver form data (medical and liability acknowledgments)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm trial bookings and send reminders
  • To process and store signed waivers securely
  • To communicate class updates and important notices

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent your data
  • We may share information with authorized staff and coaches for class management
  • We may share information with emergency services or medical professionals if required by law

4. Data Security

  • We store information in secure, access-controlled systems
  • Only authorized RRT personnel may access your personal data

5. Your Choices

You may request to review, update, or delete your data at any time by contacting us at rhythmicribbonoftexas@gmail.com

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will post changes with the date at the top of this page.
