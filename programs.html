Preschool Program

Welcome to a world where your child's imagination soars as high as their jumps! Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas is thrilled to present our Rhythmic Gymnastics Preschool Program, where students ages 3 and up can cartwheel, dance, and twirl into a bright future. Our program is lovingly designed to enrich the lives of little ones through the benefits of rhythmic gymnastics while providing nurturing care that parents can trust.

Program Overview

Our Preschool Program welcomes preschoolers aged 3 to 5, guiding them on an adventure filled with rhythm and grace. Delivered in a safe, supportive, and structured environment, our program runs Monday through Friday, with flexible scheduling options to accommodate your family's needs. Our certified and passionate instructors are dedicated to nurturing your child's physical, developmental, and social skills through the joy of movement.

What Your Child Will Learn

  • Balance & Coordination: Developing body awareness and control through fun gymnastics activities
  • Strength & Flexibility: Gaining physical strength and improving flexibility with gentle exercises tailored for young children
  • Creativity & Expression: Encouraging personal expression and creativity through dance and choreography

Beginners Program

Our beginner's program is tailored to introduce young gymnasts, typically aged 5 and up, to the basics of rhythmic gymnastics. Here's what you need to know:

Duration and Frequency

This is a year-round program, with classes held once or twice weekly. Each session lasts 90 minutes.

Instructors

All sessions are led by experienced and certified rhythmic gymnastics coaches.

Curriculum

  • Basic body movements (jumps, pivots, balances)
  • Introduction to handling apparatus (rope, hoop, ball, clubs, ribbon)
  • Development of flexibility, strength, and hand-eye coordination
  • Basic ballet concepts

Goals of the Beginner's Program

  • Instill a love for rhythmic gymnastics
  • Develop basic skills and understanding of the sport
  • Enhance physical fitness and promote healthy lifestyle choices
  • Prepare gymnasts for advanced levels, should they choose to continue

Enrollment Information

  • Requirements: No prior gymnastics experience is necessary. Suitable for children aged 5 and older.
  • Registration: Interested parents can register their children by contacting our office via phone or email. Registration is open year-round.
  • Fees: Information regarding program fees and payment options is available upon request.

Competitive Rhythmic Gymnastics

The Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas Competitive Rhythmic Gymnastics Program is designed for gymnasts who have progressed beyond beginner levels and want to compete at local, national, or even international levels.

Program Structure

  • Age Groups: Gymnasts aged 6+, segmented by age and skill level under USAG guidelines
  • Training Frequency: Multiple times a week, 4+ hours per week
  • Advanced apparatus techniques (hoop, ball, clubs, ribbon, rope)
  • Body difficulty elements
  • Ballet and dance for gymnasts
  • Strength, flexibility, and conditioning exercises
  • Choreography and routine construction

Goals and Objectives

  • Skill Development: Advance technique and mastery over the five apparatuses
  • Performance Readiness: Prepare for competition through mock meets and routine refinement
  • Mental Preparation: Focus, stress management, and performance enhancement
  • Teamwork and Sportsmanship: Foster a supportive team environment

Competitions

  • Local and Invitational Championships
  • State, Regional, and USAG Championships
  • National and International Competitions (by qualification)

Requirements for Joining

  • By invitation only
  • Evaluation required for appropriate level placement
  • High commitment expected from gymnast and parents
  • USAG Membership required

Multi-Sport Flexibility

Multi-Sport Flexibility is a stretch class developed by Coach Ella that teaches you how to articulate your body in ways that elongate muscles from end-to-end. You will learn exercises that increase flexibility, condition muscle tone, and improve body awareness through a special mind/muscle connection.

Designed specifically for rhythmic gymnasts, artistic gymnastics, ballerinas, dancers, and ice skaters. This class grew rapidly in popularity, and other sports such as swimming, football, karate, and wrestling are beginning to implement it in their training.

Benefits

  • Increases flexibility
  • Improves posture and lines
  • Improves performance quality
  • Conditions the body to prevent injuries
  • Great tool for any sport

Why Stretch?

  • Manages muscle imbalances from growing bodies
  • Important part of injury prevention
  • Deliberate lengthening of muscles to increase flexibility
  • Decreases risk of injury and muscle soreness

Private Stretching Sessions

Private stretching sessions benefit individuals of all ages and fitness levels. Helpful for muscle maintenance or rehabilitation from soreness or injury.

All Levels Welcome

The class is broken into sections, each focusing on different body parts. All levels are welcome!

Ballet

Classical ballet - widely considered to be the foundation of dance - blends tradition, discipline, and grace all into one class. At Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas, our ballet classes include elements from some of the most well-regarded styles such as Vaganova and Balanchine.

Benefits of Ballet for Young Kids

  • Learn to follow instructions
  • Develop understanding of music and rhythm
  • Improve body awareness and physical self-control
  • Correct poor alignment or posture
  • Gain a sense of discipline
  • Learn coordination, balance, and body control
  • Develop long, lean, and strong muscles
  • Gain self-confidence and pride
  • Useful for other forms of dancing and gymnastics
