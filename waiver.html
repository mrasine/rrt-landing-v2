Policies (Initial Each)

Annual Membership Fee (camp excluded): A $90 annual membership fee is due at registration. If you are a current member and your membership is not due, you will not be charged. This fee renews every 12 months for active students.
Payment Information: Tuition is due each month on the 26th, no later than the 1st. Late fees: $25 after the 5th, $50 after the 10th, and $75 after the 20th. Returned check fee: $30. Participation may be suspended if the account is not current. PayPal is accepted with a $15 processing fee (rhythmicribbonoftexas@gmail.com). Online payment may also be available via QuickBooks.
Cancellation Policy: To avoid charges for the next month, a drop class form must be submitted at least 30 days prior (beginner and recreational classes) and 60 days prior (competitive team and adult fitness). Drop forms available by email: rhythmicribbonoftexas@gmail.com.
Make-Up Classes: Contact Mrs. Rasin to schedule a make-up class for beginner classes. No make-up classes are offered for competitive program athletes.
Closures: No classes on major holidays: January 1, Memorial Day, July 4 week, Labor Day, Thanksgiving (Thu–Fri), Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, New Year’s Eve, and during inclement weather as determined by RRT/ISA.
Pick-Up Policy: Students must be picked up inside the building promptly after class. Staff is not responsible for unattended children before or after class times unless prior arrangements have been made. If a child is not picked up within 30 minutes after closure and we cannot reach a parent/guardian, staff may contact law enforcement to help ensure safe transport.
