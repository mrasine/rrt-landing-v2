Our Coaches

Meet the experienced team dedicated to helping your child succeed in rhythmic gymnastics.

Ella Rasin

Founder & Head Coach

Coach Ella is a certified rhythmic gymnastics coach with over 15 years of experience in the sport. She founded Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas to share her passion for this beautiful art form with the McKinney community.

Her competitive background includes training at elite facilities and competing in national competitions. She believes in nurturing each student's unique potential while building confidence through movement.

Credentials

  • USAG Certified Coach
  • CPR & First Aid Certified
  • 15+ Years Experience
  • Competitive Gymnast
